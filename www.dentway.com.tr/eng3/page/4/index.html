How can a Smile Design be Achieved?
Nowadays, ‘’Hollywood smile’’ concept is setting the standard. Moreover, people are consulting us because they just don't feel confident and attractive with their smiles. The latest fashion is Hollywood smile design. This model usually consists of an ab-solute symmetry of your teeth. The smallest asymmetry disturbs the look. A great smile starts with total harmony between each individual tooth. The two teeth in the middle seem the longest when we look at a person frontally. Progressively, we should clearly see, that the teeth are gradually and gracefully getting shorter.

The shape of teeth is best harmonized with each patient's face shape and lips. Each individual profile is studied and assessed before our aesthetic work begins. For ex-ample, the thickness and shape of the lower lip is very important. It provides crucial information about the dimensions of the two upper teeth. The shape of each tooth, much like each person's face, has a unique character. Oval-shaped teeth can be profiled for a person with oval face, to give one example. However, at the same time, there are limits! Sharply angled teeth don't serve the patient aesthetically or functionally, for instance. It is a delicate balance! Those with strong, angular features--like a strong jaw with a clearly defined chin--benefit the most from a slightly angular profile to their teeth.

Beside the shape, teeth color plays an important role. We shouldn’t always think that whitest colors are always the best. Teeth color should be assessed according to the skin color, gender, age, and occupation of each person, and even takes into consideration their preferred color tone. At Dentway, our service relies on striking a balance between the dentist's experience, and the patient's preferences.

Taking into consideration each patient's prior aesthetic features, the angling of teeth forward and backward is best aligned with one's lip fullness, thickness, coloring, and overall shape. The gender and expectations of the patient are crucial when arranging such angling.

All in all, following these rules makes for a great Hollywood smile experience.

What is Aesthetic Dentistry?
Aesthetic dentistry is a field that can apply to all adults. These days, many adults can see benefits from having an aesthetic dentistry consultation. Thanks to new improvements in the field, significant visual and functional improvements can be seen with the help of minimally invasive techniques. The results can create a whole new outlook!

Aesthetic dentistry can provide a quick remedy for depression as well. Women often visit their hairdressers when they're feeling down, but a new trend is to visit their dentists. It is possible to make a quick improvement of self-confidence by restoring your smile.

Dental aesthetics is a prerequisite for face aesthetics. When people meet for the first time, they quickly get their strongest impression from the eyes. Immediately after, they subconsciously evaluate the front teeth. These teeth offer a window into the person's character.

Teeth Whitening: How Is It Done?
Teeth Whitening are performed in two ways:

At-Home Whitening;
This procedure is completed by the patient at home. To start, our dental staff makes impressions of the patient's teeth, in order to create custom trays. The patient then applies safe, low-dose carbamide peroxide gel to the trays, in order to isolate the ef-fect. The desired level of tooth whiteness can be achieved in five to seven days.

Office Whitening;
This is a simple and painless procedure that can be achieved in our dental office within an hour. A high-concentration gel is used for this bleaching method. There-fore, very fast whitening can be achieved in just one session. The higher dose pre-sents no negative effects or harm to the patient.

What Is Zirconium Implant?
What is the zirconium implant that has gained popularity with the latest developments in implant technology? Our patients frequently ask us questions such as "What is the use of zirconium implant," or "why should I prefer zirconium implant?"

The dental implant, which has been in use for nearly 50 years,  has become popular in Turkey in the 2000s. Implant treatment has contributed significantly to the prestige of dentists. This is mostly because the dentists who follow the latest technology have successfully applied this treatment. Also, in this way, dentists could distinguish themselves from dental technicians who worked illegally. In the early period, the success rate of implant treatments was slightly low. It was about 50%. There was even no information about what could happen in the long run. Over the years, it was understood that the success of implant treatments should not be considered short-term. Because, when first applied, the implant could adhere to the bone and remain in the mouth, but, in the long term, it could be separated from the bone. Sometimes, despite the adherence to the bone, fractures in the neck area of the implant began to develop.

As a result, dental implants started to be evaluated in terms of short and long term performance and stability in the mouth. Later, new zirconium implants emerged as a factor that increased success. Since zirconium is a material that is very resistant to fracture, the neck fractures that developed in the long term were removed.

Zirconium implants are especially successful when the narrow implant is used in the lower jaw where the bone is thin. The material successfully maintains its integrity for many years. No neck fracture occurs. Its compatibility with porcelain coatings is excellent. Zirconium is a tissue-friendly material, and in the long term, there is no incompatibility with the gums and bones.

For the reasons mentioned above, zirconium implants should be preferred as much as possible. Zirconium implant treatment is slightly more expensive than other implants. There are only a few manufacturers. But other brands are also working on zirconium production.
